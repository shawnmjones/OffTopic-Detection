Press Releases : US ISSUES HUMAN RIGHTS REPORT ON NAMIBIA
The US Government (USG) on May 31 2012 released its annual report on human rights practices worldwide, including Namibia. According to the Report, Namibia experiences three predominant human rights abuses in the area of civil and political rights during 2011. These include police use of excessive force, poor detention center conditions, and violence and discrimination against women and children, including rape, child abuse, and child labor.
