Press Releases: BROTHER CONFESSES TO SISTER’S MURDER

By: LUQMAN CLOETE at LÜDERITZ*

 THE brother and a close relative of Thobeka Loretta Somi (35), accused of raping and murdering Somi in her home in Rosh Pinah last Tuesday, made their first appearance in the Lüderitz Magistrate’s Court yesterday. Arraigned on charges of rape and murder during an in-camera court session, Somi’s biological brother, Mbulelo Mashac Somi (21), pleaded guilty to the murder charge. He however, denied raping her. Somi’s brother told the court a fight started between him and his sister after a quarrel over money she allegedly owed him. He claimed he fought back in self defence after his sister slapped him in the face.
