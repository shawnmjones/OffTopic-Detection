Nanda argues that asanas, even pranayama, are not found in the Vedas. Inconveniently for her, the Maitrayaniya Upanishad presents a six-limbed discipline, including pranayama, virtually identical to Patanjali’s Ashtanga Yoga. She argues that while BKS Iyengar taught some 200 asanas, Pradipika listed only 15. Indeed, not all 200 existed at first, but a clear trend of development exists.
