FAQ
The Egypt Electionnaire is a project by the Arabic Network for Human Rights Information (ANHRI)  and MICT (Media in Cooperation and Transition)
But which party is yours? What are the parties’ real  positions? Understand the positions of the main parties and find out  which party best matches your own opinions!
 
Attention: The results are based on the answers provided by one spokesperson of each of the political parties.The answers of the parties for the Electionnaire might differ from other political statements announced in contexts, such as public speeches or newspaper interviews. You  are, of course, not supposed to vote just according to this result.   Rather, the tool is meant to offer you greater background information   before you go to the ballot box.
